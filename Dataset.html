A million Headlines Dataset

This Dataset was extracted from ABC news. It includes the date of publication and headlines from 2003-2020

With this dataset I would make a timeline over the past decade that shows how the media (ABC) reports stories over time.

It would be a good idea to find if other media outlets have headlines data availbale too, to compare how different publications report certain topics.

Overall, the visualizations would highligth trends in stories along with a timeline to give context of these events. (i.e, presidential elections, global emergencies, olympics coverage, etc.)
